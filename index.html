The important boundary

Evaluate the system,
not just a demo.

This is designed for arbitrary user-created PAL configurations. A research workflow, a publishing agent, and a support chatbot should not receive the same test; they should be held to the same rigor.

The live workspace runs are calibration samples. They prove the eval can observe PAL; they are not a claim that every PAL configuration performs the same way.

03 / What gets measured

✓

Success

Did the run meet every blocking acceptance criterion?

✦

Quality

Correctness, completeness, adherence, and actionability.

◌

Cost

Tokens, tool calls, sub-agents, and cost per successful task.

↯

Latency

First event, completion time, and p50/p95 over repeat runs.

⊘

Safety

Privacy, refusal quality, least privilege, and external action gates.

≈

Stability

Outcome variance across paraphrases and repeat runs.
